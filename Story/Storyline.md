## Plot Synopsis

### Premise - Intro Lore Frames
Ryan Chessman is a college student graduating into a crazy world. Destroyed supply chains and armed conflicts, peace is rarer than disarray. Such extenuating circumstances has most importantly has impacted chess as king and queen production has been essentially become impossible. But there is hope, as pieces like the pawn remain available worldwide. 

This has lead to the creation of pawn chess, a game so new, there are few common rules. Its yours, Ryan's, mission to keep  chess alive at this time by unifying pawn chess under one ruleset and become the world pawn chess champion.
