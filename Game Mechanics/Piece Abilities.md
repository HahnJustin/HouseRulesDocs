*This is currently a knowledge dump page for tracking, we will likely build it out more later.*

- [[Weak Pawn]]: No En Passant
- [[Normal Pawn]]: En Passant 
- [[Poison Pawn]]: Attacking Pawn dies if captured
- [[Knight]]: Moves like a Knight
- [[Inverse Pawn]]: Moves diagonally, captures vertically
- [[Glass Pawn]]: Dies when capturing
- [[Wall Piece]]: Moves any direction, but can only block, can't win
- [[Golden Calf]]: Knight, but can win
