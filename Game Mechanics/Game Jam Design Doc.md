# 2026 Backeys Game Jam
## Executive Summary
### Game Overview 
You are an amateur psuedo-chess player and you decide to start competing against other players in hope to become champion of the world of psuedo-chess. Problem is every country seems to have their own house rules… I hope they’re aren’t too strange.

### High Concept 
Effectively make a simple game more fun by adding silly rules and environments. Somethings thats simple enough to learn quickly and complicated enough at the end to inspire people to keep playing.

- Web-based
