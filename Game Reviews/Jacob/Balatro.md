# The Review 
Amazing game, it's Balatro. insanely replayable, interesting variants that make for hundreds of hours of gameplay. 

### What the game does well mechanically?
The game builds on itself insanely well. The ability to make simple changes but 1,000,000x score and have challenging bars scaling similarly is not an easy feat. The difference choise and amount of adventure, amazing.
