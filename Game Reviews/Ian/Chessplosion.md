### Description
Chessplosion is an arcade action puzzle game where you drop chess piece bombs to create chain reactions and trap opponents. Defeat enemies and bosses in Adventure Mode and the roguelike-inspired Dungeon Mode, break targets with limited bombs in Puzzle Mode, or fight friends and AI in Battle Mode.

# The Review - I loved it!
Fire... great game and it has a lot going on that is fun. Has so much to offer and definitely was a long term project that they cared about.

### What the game does well mechanically?
This is a big game and looks and feels very pro! Music is excellent! Drum and Bass! Many many game modes and even online play. It looks like a long term project.

### What could it improve on? / What did I not like
Nothing... no notes

### What the game does well in marketing?

- supports a lot of languages!
- very positive rating!
- blogs a lot
- youtube videos on preview or trailer
- self-published!

### After playing am I surprised by how much it made (or didn't)?
I am surprised how much it didn't make... This is an example of a pro game dev that didn't much out there. Maybe because they are self published? I am not sure but it is hard to compare our game because it is a movable character setting down chess pieces that explode to kill enemies. Not the same at all. Makes me think of other games that end up in this situation more often than other games doing well?

### How does it relate to possible implementations of our game?
I think it is very different and very original in the sense that you have a movable character dropping bombs that are chess piece capture shapes.
