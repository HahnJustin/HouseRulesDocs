1. [Get Screen to Gif](https://www.screentogif.com/)

2.  Make sure you know the limitations of the platform you want to post to, almost all platforms have a size (in mb) or time limit for gifs.
	* Steam has a 12s time limit on gifs for store pages
	* Discord has like a 10mb file limit overall
	* Itch has a 3mb limit on thumbnail gifs
	* Add more here

5. Click the two gear icon and make sure that you are not recording the mouse

 6. Do your edits
